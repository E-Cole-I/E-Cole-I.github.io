Quick Bio

Geography and technology enthusiast with a passion for problem solving. I built this portfolio to practice my web development skills!

I love technology and the unique ways people are leveraging it to tackle problems. WIth technology rapidly evolving and data availability increasing there will be an increasing demand for individuals with skills to tackle these types of problems. This is extremely exciting from multiple angles whether it’s a career path or a hobby. There are and will be many opportunities to develop advanced skills in the field of data.

My academic career has helped me realize the power of geographic information science and the place it has in the world of technology. The traditional view of GIS is continuously adapting and changing from an application based domain to more system based. With the uniqueness of spatial data in terms of complexity, many data challenges are elevated with incorporating spatial information. My interests in GIS are in enterprise solutions that encompass geographic problems and integrate it seamlessly with non-spatial information.

My favorite tools right now include Python, Postgresql (PostGIS), JavaScript, and NodeJS. I'm currently in the early stages of learning MongoDB, ElasticSearch, AWS Platform, and Apache Spark! I will update this page as new and cool projects emerge.
